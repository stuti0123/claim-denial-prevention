BILLED AMOUNT POLICY — Financial Requirements for Claim Submissions
=====================================================================
Document ID: POL-BA-001
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Department: Revenue Cycle Management

Section 1: Purpose
-------------------
This policy establishes the requirements for the billed amount field
on healthcare claim submissions. The billed amount is a mandatory
financial field required for claim processing and reimbursement.

Section 2: Billed Amount Requirements
----------------------------------------
Every healthcare claim MUST include a billed amount. The billed
amount represents the total charge for the medical service provided.

Requirements:
  - The billed amount must be a positive numeric value.
  - The billed amount must reflect the actual cost of the service.
  - The billed amount should align with the provider's published
    fee schedule.
  - Zero-dollar claims are only acceptable for no-charge services
    that are pre-approved by the payer.

Section 3: Missing Amount Flag — WARN_MISSING_AMOUNT
------------------------------------------------------
When a claim is submitted without a billed amount, it is flagged
with WARN_MISSING_AMOUNT. This flag carries a risk weight of 1.

A missing billed amount indicates:
  - The claim was submitted before the billing department completed
    the charge capture process.
  - A system error may have dropped the amount during electronic
    submission.
  - The service may not have been properly coded in the provider's
    billing system.

Remediation steps for WARN_MISSING_AMOUNT:
  a) Verify the charge in the provider's billing system or
     electronic health record (EHR).
  b) Calculate the correct billed amount using the provider's
     fee schedule for the CPT code on the claim.
  c) Enter the billed amount and resubmit the claim.
  d) If the service was provided at no charge, document the
     reason and submit with a zero-dollar amount and a no-charge
     authorization code.

Section 4: Amount Validation Against Expected Cost
-----------------------------------------------------
After the billed amount is populated, it will be validated against
the expected cost for the procedure. Claims where the billed amount
exceeds 3.0 times the expected cost will be flagged with
WARN_HIGH_BILLING. See the Billing Guidelines (POL-BG-001) for
details on high billing thresholds and remediation.
